YOUR PARTNER FOR SIMPLISITY TRANSPORTATION

Autrona UAB, which started its operations in 1998, can offer its customers high-quality logistics services throughout Europe and the CIS countries that meet the customer's needs. By fulfilling our obligations responsibly, we have earned the trust of regular customers and partners.

We are proud that UAB "Autrona" has received the "Gazelle of the Year" award for several years in a row. Since 1998, the company has been a member of the Lithuanian National Association of Motor Carriers.

Provide efficient and professional logistics services - safely, qualitatively and quickly deliver your goods to their destination. Conduct activities with a responsible approach to the environment and using the latest technologies.
Our vision:
As reliable and innovative partners, we work to become part of your success story.
Our greatest strength
is a team of professional employees and constant investments in new and modern vehicles, because only this ensures the quality and efficiency of the services provided.
